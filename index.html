    2014.9-?

    Thangka Relief Generation
    We present a new practical approach for generating Thangka relief from Thangka line drawing. We separate the input image with flood fill and Bezier curves first and then apply discrete Poisson equation for region inflation due to the smoothness of the relief surface. Unlike previous techniques, our system allows continuous surface adjustment automatically or manually with the observation of the surface complexity of Thangka relief. For each separated region, we adjust the relief surface with chamfer distance transform as parameters of discrete Poisson equation and take advantage of GPU computing to solve the equation iteratively. With all the regions generated, we merge them and obtain the final Thangka relief.

    2015.9-2016.7

    Deep Spherical Panoramic Representation for 3D Shape Recognition
    We propose a 3D model recognition method based on convolutional neural networks (CNN). Given a shape, it generates spherical panoramas using spherical depth projection, and then achieve recognition of the 3D model by recognizing those panoramas with a CNN. The innovation point is that we unfold a sphere projection on various orientations to produce multiple panoramas.
